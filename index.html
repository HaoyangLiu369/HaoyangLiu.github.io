Financial NLP

Does Financial News Sentiment Move the S&P 500?

DATA 498D capstone project testing whether financial news headline sentiment helps predict next-day S&P 500 movement under different market regimes.

  • Compared dictionary sentiment, VADER, FinBERT, and topic features.
  • Modeled direction with Logistic Regression and XGBoost.
  • Evaluated out-of-sample predictions with backtesting and regime analysis.

Classification

Bank Marketing Term Deposit Prediction

Built a realistic pre-call prediction workflow for the UCI Bank Marketing dataset, focused on whether a client will subscribe to a term deposit.

  • Removed call duration to avoid target leakage.
  • Compared Logistic Regression, LASSO, Naive Bayes, Random Forest, XGBoost, and PCA-based models.
  • Used cross-validated threshold tuning for imbalanced classification.

Climate Analytics

Climate Prediction via Sparse Group Lasso

Used Sparse Group Lasso for ultra-high-dimensional climate prediction, selecting important Pacific grid cells for Shanghai monthly temperature modeling.

  • Processed 935 monthly samples with 1,295 climate predictors.
  • Grouped five climate variables within each spatial grid cell.
  • Compared SGL with Ridge, XGBoost, and LightGBM for accuracy and interpretability.
